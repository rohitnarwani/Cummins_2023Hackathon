Steps To Reduce Your Carbon Footprints

The next several years will be critical in our fight against climate change, and our capacity to reduce our carbon footprint will undoubtedly determine our success.

  • Suistanable Agriculture

    Practice carbon sequestration, reduce use of fossil fuels and synthetics fertilizers and follow traditional farming methods.

  • Sustainable Mode of Transportation

    Start adopting sustainable modes of transportation, such as public transportation, bicycles, or walking, and purchase ecologically friendly vehicles.

  • Reduce Energy Consumption

    To save energy, choose 100% renewable energy, acquire energy-saving appliances, and control heating and air conditioning.

  • Awareness About Carbon Footprint

    Make others aware of the need of decreasing one's carbon footprint.

  • Reuse Reduce Recycle

    Reduce waste: Reuse your packaging, recycle it and if this is not possible, dispose of it in the appropriate container.
