1. Eat low on the food chain like fruits, veggies, grains, beans.
2.Choose organic and local foods that are in season to reduce transportation.
3.Buy foodstuff in bulk when possible using your own reusable container.
1. If you’re shopping for a new car, consider purchasing a hybrid or electric vehicle.
2. If you must drive, avoid unnecessary braking and acceleration.
3. Drive less. Walk, take public transportation, carpool, rideshare or bike to your destination when possible.
1. Make an effort to minimise your electricity usage.
2.Switch off lights when not in that room.
3.Sign up to get your electricity from clean energy through your local utility or a certified renewable energy provider.
1.Make an effort to separate your plastic, paper, metal and glass waste in your home.
2.Choose to use paper shopping bags, metal straws and plastic alternatives where possible.
3.Opt for low-phosphate products as these are better for water systems, rivers and the ocean.
